Cream Che​ese Oreo

Want to know how to make an easy Cream Cheese Oreo?

Prepare the materials we need and let's make it! Follow the instructions that I have given in this blog. I've written the recipe as easy as possible. So I believe you can make it too!

The materials we need are ...

1. 250 ml milk
2. 2 packs of Oreo
3. 50 g flour
4. Cheese
5. Butter

6. Refined sugar
7. A cup or bowl
8. Cheese grater
9. Blender
10. Pan and stove

How to make Cream Cheese Oreo?

1. Separate the oreo biscuits and oreo cream.
2. Crush the oreo biscuits with a blender. Don't be too smooth, just enough.
3. Grate the cheese sufficiently.
4. Put the flour then add the milk. Stir until well blended.
5. Turn on the stove over low heat and melt the butter. Then pour in the milk and stir until evenly mixed.

6. If it feels enough,  add the cheese litte by little and stir. Then add flour little by little and stir until cream is lumpy.
7. Wait for 5-10 minutes and let the cream cool first.
8. Add cream and arrange to make it look beautiful. Then put in the oreo biscuits shards earlier. Do this alternately repeatedly until it is full.
9. Close the container and put it in the refrigerator. Wait for 1 day.
10. Finally, a delicious cream cheese oreo is ready to be enjoyed.
